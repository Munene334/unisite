Welcome to Lennox University

The Lennox University, Kenya's largest and oldest university, has been a beacon of higher education since its founding in 1902.
It offers a diverse range of programs across numerous faculties, and is committed to scholarly excellence, research, and community service.

Research and innovation

At the Lennox University, cutting-edge research is underway in the Department of Engineering, focusing on sustainable energy systems and advanced robotics.
This work aims to address global challenges in environmental sustainability and automation, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration to drive technological innovation.
Researchers are actively developing novel solutions with real-world applications, from enhancing renewable energy efficiency to creating intelligent autonomous agents.

New breakthroughs in wind turbine blade design are dramatically increasing efficiency and reducing maintenance costs for wind energy farms.

Advanced robotics research is focusing on developing highly autonomous, adaptable, and collaborative robots capable of performing complex tasks in unpredictable environments.

The Department of Engineering is a hub for innovative research and education, pushing the boundaries of technological advancement across diverse disciplines.

Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, admission requirements and financial aid options

Our university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across faculties such as Arts and Humanities, Science, Engineering, Business, Health Sciences, and Social Sciences. Specialized certificate and diploma courses are also available.
Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, undergraduate applicants need to submit academic transcripts, standardized test scores (if applicable), and proof of English proficiency. Postgraduate programs may require a bachelor's degree, recommendation letters, and a statement of purpose.
Yes, we provide on-campus housing for both domestic and international students. Options range from dormitory-style rooms to apartment units, and all include access to communal facilities such as kitchens, study rooms, and laundry services.
Our university is committed to fostering an inclusive and multicultural environment. We host numerous cultural events, have active diversity clubs, and provide resources and training aimed at equity and inclusion for all students, faculty, and staff.
Absolutely. The university offers a variety of merit-based and need-based scholarships, grants, and work-study programs. International students are also eligible for select awards. Financial aid applications should be submitted early due to high demand.
We offer comprehensive support including academic advising, career counseling, mental health services, tutoring centers, and assistance for students with disabilities. Our goal is to ensure students thrive both academically and personally.
